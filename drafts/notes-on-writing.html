Notes on writing

Sun 01 November 2015

Conveying meaning can be hard. Make it easier by writing simply; shorten obscure pronouncements big words and delete excess verbage useless sentences. Just write like you talk, damnit.

Start with the problem, not the solution. Make people understand the headache before pointing out the asprin. Then again, who is the audience? If they've googled their problem, they probably have the headache already.
