Conveying meaning can be hard.  Make it easier by writing simply;
shorten ~~obscure pronouncements~~ big words and delete ~~excess verbage~~
useless sentences.  Just
[write like you talk](http://www.paulgraham.com/talk.html), damnit.


Start with the problem, not the solution.  Make people understand the
[headache before pointing out the asprin](http://mkremins.github.io/blog/doors-headaches-intellectual-need/).
Then again, who is the audience?  If they've googled their problem, they
probably have the headache already.
